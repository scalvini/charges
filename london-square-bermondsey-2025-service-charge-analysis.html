Methodology

Private = Private Internal charge + Private Apartment charge combined (+ Roof Terrace for The Crosse). Tannery: £124,228 on 70,808 sqft. Crosse: £152,266 on 105,508 sqft.

Affordable = Affordable Internal charge only. Tannery: £66,228 on 15,429 sqft. Crosse: £54,676 on 14,747 sqft.

Building charge is excluded from category charts as it covers shared building-wide costs. It is included only in the final "total cost per sqft" chart, allocated proportionally by sqft.

Sqft figures are from lease schedules. Private sqft = Private Internal + Private Apartment areas combined.
